How to use

Cut cards, or hand out named slips. Split into the two rooms shown above.

  1. Choose a playset and player count (add names if you want).
  2. Shuffle — roles are randomized and rooms are split evenly.
  3. In spoiler-safe mode, pass the phone/laptop so each person reveals only their slip.
  4. Or print: cut character cards (and backs), use leader cards + hostage chart.
  5. Play physically — no Wi‑Fi, PeerJS, or color-reveal browser bugs required.
