How to use

Cut, shuffle physically if you want, deal facedown, then split into rooms.

  1. Set the player count and optional advanced roles.
  2. Hit shuffle — the site builds a legal deck and randomizes order.
  3. Print on letter/A4 paper, cut along the guides, keep backs identical.
  4. Deal one card per player. Separate into two rooms as evenly as possible.
  5. Use the printed leader cards and hostage chart during rounds.
